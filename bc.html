Breast Cancer

Breast cancer is a kind of cancer that begins as a growth of cells in the breast tissue. After skin cancer, breast cancer is the most common cancer diagnosed in women in the United States

Dieatry Option

1.Broccoli: Contains glucosinolates, which may help protect against breast cancer.

2.Cauliflower: Similar to broccoli, it offers the benefits of glucosinolates.

3.Kale: A nutrient-dense leafy green that can provide antioxidants and fibre.

4.Cabbage: Another member of the cruciferous family with potential benefits.

5. Blueberries: Rich in antioxidants, including anthocyanins.

Lifestyle Changes:

 Exercise Regularly: Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise most days of the week.

 Strength Training: Include strength training exercises to maintain muscle mass.

 Relaxation Techniques: Practice relaxation techniques like yoga, meditation, or deep breathing.

 Support Groups: Connect with others who understand your experience.

 Follow-upAppointments:Attend regular follow ups with your health care provider.
